ABOUT
Andrew Yuen is a cinematographer and filmmaker currently attending American High School and is based in Fremont, California. He creates miscellaneous videos, short films, and various tutorials on editing and camerawork. Through his work, he aims to make something meaningful as well as share his joy of making videos with others. It’s also really fun. Many of his pieces deal with personal topics as a form of self expression. Others are much more lighthearted e.g. little editing experiments or fun comedic bits. No matter the flavor of the video, Andrew’s sharp and distinct editing flair always exudes from its frames.
